About Us

Teleconformity is an independent company, dedicated to assist companies in the Electronics, IT and Radio & Telecommunication Industry with obtaining regulatory Certificates and Approvals required to place products on the market. Teleconformity is specialized in Radio, Telecom, EMC and Safety certification, and manages your FCC, Industry Canada, European R&TTE/EMC/LVD and Japanese MIC ("TELEC, JATE") certification as well approvals for most other countries (worldwide). We are your one-stop consulting, certification and approval partner. »»

Experience

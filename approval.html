NORTH-AMERICA

United State, Mexico

Country Details
United states
● RF and telecom certification: The US FCC (Federal Communications Commission) approval can be granted either directly by the FCC or by an accredited TCB (Telecommunication Certification Body), depending on the technology.
● Testing or Accept FCC/CE reports: A TCB is a private company which has obtained proper accreditations to certify certain RF products, against FCC standards.
● Product safety approvals : Industry Canada, has a similar process to the FCC. An FCB (Foreign Certification Body), a private company, is authorized to review test reports, product documentation and grant approvals, based on compliance with Industry Canada Standards.
Mexico RF/Telecom/Safety Approval
● RF and telecom certification: COFETEL (Comisi Federal de Telecomunicaciones, or Federal Telecommunications Commission)
● Local Testing or Accept FCC/CE reports: FCC/CE reports are accepted.
● Product safety approvals : The NOM (Normas Oficiales Mexicanas, or Mexican Official Standards) product safety approval is also required for certain products.
● Local representative: A local distributor is required and each importer/local dealer must have approval issued in its name.

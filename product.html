U9507 Series Introduction

U9507 series is a LTE CAT4 cellular module based on QUALCOMM MDM9X07,which is designed for the global IOT and M2M market. With 3GPP Rel.11, U9507 series can provide 150Mbps data downlink and 50Mbps uplink. It is a competitive product with high performance and good price. It has some Key Feature as following:
· Small Size with LCC package
· High Reliability
· Wide Work Temperature
· Multi-constellation GNSS
· Support FOTA, eCall*, DTMF and VoLTE
· Support OpenCPU
To match different area and country complicated LTE band requirement, U9507 has 4 variants: U9507C for China & U9507W for Asia ; U9507E for Europe; U9507A for American. It also support MiniPCIe Interface by a converting PCB.
